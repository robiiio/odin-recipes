Burger

Description

The term "burger" is a more general term that refers to any sandwich made with a patty of ground meat. Burgers can be made with beef, chicken, pork, turkey, or even vegetables.

A burger is a patty of ground beef grilled and placed between two halves of a bun. Slices of raw onion, lettuce, bacon, mayonnaise, and other ingredients add flavor. Burgers are considered an American food but are popular around the world.

Steps

  1. Set out a large mixing bowl and add in the ground beef, crushed crackers, egg, Worcestershire sauce, milk, and spices.
  2. Press the meat down in the bowl, into an even disk
  3. Mix all our ingredients together
  4. Preheat the grill or a skillet to medium heat, approximately 350-400 degrees F.
  5. Grill or fry the patties for 3-4 minutes per side for a medium burger.
  6. Put the grilled or the fried burger in our bread
  7. Add some topings like salads and ketchup with mayonnaise
